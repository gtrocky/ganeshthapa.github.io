Short Bio

I am an economist with a Ph.D. in Agricultural Economics (Purdue University). I work at the Wisconsin Department of Natural Resources, where I conduct socio-economic impact analyses, economic modeling, and policy evaluations related to environmental and natural resource management. My research focuses on wildlife disease economics, water resources and property values, and agricultural transformation.
