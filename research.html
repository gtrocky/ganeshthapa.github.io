Research

My work often blends econometrics, spatial data, and policy questions. A few areas I’m excited about:

  • Wildlife disease economics. Understanding the short‑ and medium‑term impacts of chronic wasting disease (CWD) on hunting participation and local economies; evaluating policy responses and behavioral shifts.
  • Water & property values. Linking observed lake level changes to assessed values and transactions in waterfront markets; exploring risk perceptions and adaptation.
  • Agricultural transformation. Value chains, fertilizer policy, and labor markets with a focus on South Asia.
